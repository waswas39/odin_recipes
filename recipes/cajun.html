Cajun Chicken with Lentils and Potatoes

DESCRIPTION

Shredded cajun chicken breasts and red lentils served with a side of roasted potatoes and zucchini. This is a meal made using cheap ingredients making it perfect for those of you who need something that is budget friendly.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/4 lbs chicken breast
  • 1/2 lb red lentils dry
  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups chiccken broth
  • 1 1/2 tbsp cajun seasoning
  • 1 1/2 lb russet potatoes
  • 2 large zucchini
  • Salt and pepper
  • 5 tbsp parsley

STEPS

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F.
  2. Cut your onion, celery, and carrots into a small dice. Cut your potatoes and zucchini into a medium dice.
  3. Rinse your lentils and sift through them to pick out any debris.
  4. Add 1 tbsp of oil to a large pot and sauté the carrots, onion, celery, and garlic until they have become fragrant and softened.
  5. Add the lentils, chicken broth, tomato sauce and chicken breast. Bring to a boil and reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for 25 minutes.
  6. While the chicken is cooking, toss the potatoes in 1 tbsp of oil and 1 tbsp of Cajun seasoning. Bake for 12 minutes then turn and bake an additional 12-15 minutes or until you achieve optimal browning. Season to taste.
  7. Heat the remaining oil in a large skillet and sauté the zucchini until soft. Season to taste.
  8. Once the chicken is done, remove it from the pot and shred with two forks. Return to the pot and stir.
  9. This recipe makes 5 servings. Divide your ingredients evenly 5 ways.
